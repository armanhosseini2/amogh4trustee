The Student Platform

Free Student Transit

No student should struggle to get to class. We will push for universal bus passes across the region.

Route 13 Solutions

Unreliable wait times are unacceptable. I'll fight for increased frequency on Route 13 during peak hours.

Direct Tutoring

Shifting funds from administrative "studies" into direct peer-to-peer and professional tutoring support.

WRDSB Accountability

Cutting through the red tape. Less time in board meetings, more time focusing on classroom results.

Mental Wellness

Formalized mental health days to acknowledge the pressures modern students face every single day.

Education is the backbone of Kitchener’s future, but for too long, the voices of students and parents have been drowned out by bureaucracy. I’m running for Trustee because I believe in a WRDSB that is transparent, efficient, and relentlessly focused on student outcomes. Whether it's fixing transit or expanding academic support, my approach is simple: if it doesn't help the student in the classroom, it isn't a priority.
